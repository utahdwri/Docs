# Cory Angeroth

Since July 2025, I have served as the Water Data Service Manager within the Utah Division of Water Rights, a role that integrates my interest in water data with my expertise in hydrology. My extensive background as a Hydrologist, specializing in water monitoring and data collection, provides me with a deep understanding of water systems and the importance of reliable data. My academic journey began at the University of Arizona, where I earned a degree in Hydrology and Water Resources, fostering my appreciation for the importance of water. My connection to water began in childhood, growing up on a Midwestern lake, and has since deepened through my many years in Utah, where I enjoy the state's diverse recreational opportunities.

\
I feel a profound responsibility to provide high-quality water data to all Utah residents and stakeholders. I have a strong commitment to the availability and accuracy and water data as I understand its importance to the well-being of the state. It is a privilege to contribute to this vital mission, ensuring present and future generations of Utahns have access to the information needed to make informed decisions about this precious resource.

\
When not in the office, I enjoy all manner of outdoor activities, from skiing in the winter to golfing in the summer with plenty of time on lakes and rivers mixed in!
