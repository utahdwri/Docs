# Progress

The Team undertook a comprehensive effort to digitize and review historical water distribution data across all 43 distribution systems. This initiative involved scanning Annual Commissioner Reports (CR) from 1983 to 2024, manually entering daily, monthly, and annual measurements into the Divisionâ€™s Water Measurement database (DVRT), and conducting quality control assessments.
